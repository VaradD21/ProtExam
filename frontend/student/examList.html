Exam Rules & Instructions

📋 General Rules

  • You must complete the exam within the allocated time
  • Once submitted, you cannot change your answers
  • Any violation of rules may result in disqualification

📷 Proctoring Requirements

  • Camera must remain on throughout the exam
  • You must stay in fullscreen mode
  • Face must be clearly visible at all times
  • No other persons should be visible in the camera

🚫 Prohibited Actions

  • Switching tabs or applications
  • Using mobile phones or other devices
  • Looking away from the screen for extended periods
  • Opening developer tools or inspecting elements
  • Right-clicking or copying content

⚠️ Violations & Consequences

  • Warning: First violation - visual warning
  • Auto-submit: Multiple violations - exam submitted automatically
  • Disqualification: Severe violations - exam marked as invalid
